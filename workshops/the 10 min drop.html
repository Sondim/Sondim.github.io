The 10-Minute post

Endlessly tweaking a post isn't a smart marketing strategy but usually just over-editing to slow down our progress because we are trying to avoid a rough feeling, like being judged or criticized.

This workshop is fast by design to not give you enough time to overthink and instead get to the core of why you can't share your work.

It is a radical way to stop being controlled by your own fear and make it easier to promote your game!

Step 1

Gather material of your game and pick a platform to share it on

Step 2

Start the 10-minute timer and share your post

Step 3

Publish your post and sit with the rush of feelings

CONFIGURE TIMER

THE CLOCK IS TICKING

You have 10 minutes to write your post, upload your asset and hit publish on social media or Discord. You got this, write spontaneously and from your heart!

10:00

Pause & Feel

You're not hiding anymore. You're out wide open!

For the next 60 seconds, close your eyes and sit with whatever comes up. Focus on your body. Don't try to get rid of the feelings/sensations that come.

01:00

WORKSHOP COMPLETE

Great job! You did fantastic, I hope that was ok for you! If the feelings are still intense, take a deep breath and maybe shake out some tension if you need to. It will pass, the discomfort of not hiding anymore doesn't mean you've done something wrong!

Now take a mental step back and let the post be, for this particular exercise I recommend that you don't edit it.

The more you practice sharing like this, the less you will hide and the more boldly you can share you work. And the more you share your work, the more you will learn what your audience wants and the more connection you'll get with your audience. Connecting with your audience is the ultimate goal of marketing your game!
