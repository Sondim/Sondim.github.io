CONFIGURE SESSION

PHASE 1: SOMATIC WARM-UP

Write the most brutal, unfair, 1-star Steam review of your game you can imagine. Make it highly personal.

Read what you wrote out loud. Close your eyes and find where the sting lands in your body. Welcome the physical sensation of failure without trying to think your way out of it.

PHASE 2: HUMANIZE THE ENEMY

Shift your perception of an online forum from an "enemy" to "human beings" to drop hyper-vigilance. Complete these tasks in another tab.

PHASE 3: THE DROP

You have 10 minutes to post your game to social media or a discord channel. No proofreading. No aesthetic adjustments. Say "ouch" out loud to your critical thoughts, post it anyway, and walk away.

10:00

TIME'S UP. WALK AWAY.

You can’t be accepted for who you are if you’re not showing up as who you are. Notice the frantic self-talk you just survived. The screen stops feeling like a shield and starts feeling like a bridge to connection.
