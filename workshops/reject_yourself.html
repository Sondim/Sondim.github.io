REJECT YOURSELF FIRST

This workshop helps you to face the discomfort of negative feedback and be okay with the fact that some people won't like what you post.

We do it here in a safe space first with the goal to help you share your game more freely and build the audience your game deserves

Step 1

Get comfortable and prepare your environment

Step 2

Write a 1-star review/comment of your own game

Step 3

Read it out loud and face the discomfort

PREPARATIONS

THE 1 star review

Write the most brutal, unfair, 1-star Steam review or comment of your game you can imagine. Make it highly personal. Don't hold back.

Read it out loud slowly

Read what you wrote out loud and feel for the "sting"!

Accept and try to welcome the feeling and physical sensation in your body. If you lose focus and start thinking just come back to the feeling again, keep your focus there for a minute before moving on.

01:00

Workshop Complete

You did fantastic! Take a deep breath and relax, I hope that felt ok for you!

The only way to completely avoid being rejected by others is to hide, but if you want to promote your game you can't stay hidden so you need to reject yourself first. Which can sound strange but it's about allowing yourself to physically feel the hurt and discomfort of rejection in a safe space so that you can be more resilient and put your game out there!
