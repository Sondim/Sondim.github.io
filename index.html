Meet Magnus

For a decade, I worked as a physiotherapist—treating the physical toll of stress. It wasn't until a period of intense anxiety and insomnia that I discovered the world of sound as an anchor. That pivot led to a career in audio post-production and music for games.

I’ve worked in teams that lost the big picture and experienced the "crunch" of releases that left me struggling for months. These challenges taught me that the combination of great people and a healthy culture creates the best outcomes.

"I realized that deep self-discovery isn't just for a meditation cushion—it's in the work of raising money, managing a team, and launching a game."

Today, I coach founders to ensure their ambition and inner work fuel each other. In fact, that's what Sondim means: that moment when sound dims and we pause to receive our true experience.

My teachings draw from contemplative traditions, modern neuroscience, and psychology. I work with solo devs, indie teams, and executives to turn business into a practice of self-discovery and high performance.
