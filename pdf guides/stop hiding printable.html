This workshop is fast by design to bypass overthinking and cut to the core of why you struggle to share your game. The goal is to make promoting your game easier and more natural.

STEP 1

Gather material of your game and pick a platform to share it on

STEP 2

Start a 10-minute timer and write your post

STEP 3

Publish and sit with the rush of feelings

PREPARATIONS

I have a screenshot, gif, or video of my game ready to go.

I have selected the social platform or Discord server.

I have my phone or a clock ready to set for 10 minutes.

THE CLOCK IS TICKING

Start your timer for 10 minutes. You have exactly this long to write your post, upload your asset, and hit publish. Write spontaneously from the heart. Do not overthink.

PAUSE & FEEL

Once published, DO NOT check for likes. Close the tab. For the next 60 seconds, close your eyes and sit with whatever comes up in your body. Do not try to push the feelings away.

The more you practice sharing like this, the more boldly you can let yourself be seen and share you work. The more you share your work, the more you will learn what your audience wants and the more connection you'll get with your audience. Connecting with your audience is the ultimate goal of marketing your game!
